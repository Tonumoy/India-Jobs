How to use this visualizer

This treemap shows every major occupation in the Indian economy, sized by how many people work in that role and coloured by the metric you pick.

READING THE TILES

  • Bigger tile = more workers in that occupation.
  • Colour = whatever you've selected in the View toggle.
  • Click or tap a tile to see the LLM-written rationale + a link to the original source report.

THE FOUR VIEWS

  • 🤖 AI Exposure (0–10) — how much an LLM thinks AI is reshaping this work, per the India-specific rubric in make_prompt.py.
  • 📈 Hiring — recent YoY direction, from sector reports.
  • 💵 Pay — median annual gross pay in ₹.
  • 🎓 Education — typical formal qualification expected.

HOW THE SCORES ARE GENERATED

Each occupation's description (in pages/<slug>.md) is sent to an open LLM via OpenRouter along with a rubric. The LLM returns a 0-10 score and a 2-3 sentence rationale. The whole pipeline re-runs every Monday at 04:00 IST automatically. All free models, $0 cost per run.

CAVEATS

  • Scores are LLM estimates, not predictions.
  • High exposure does not mean the job disappears — it often means each worker becomes more productive.
  • Employment counts are reconciled from PLFS, NASSCOM, NSDC, ministries, regulators, and company filings.
